J.O TEAM 团队介绍

JOEY 邹毅

将艺术与实践吻合并呈现商业附加值，我们需要明确自己的创作目标和设计手法，团队也需要明确自己应该如何用心成就项目，如何全面成为自己，如何真正成为设计顾问；因此我们需要将艺术的层面不断突破与更新，并对设计的理念保持执着，追求于形态和实际带来的效果，让设计的形式可以多元化。

In order to match art with practice and present commercial added value, we need to define our creative goals and design techniques, and the team will also learn how to focus on achieving projects, how to be themselves and how to become a real design consultant; Therefore, we need to keep breaking and updating the levels of our design art and scope; Stick to the idea of our design, pursue the effect of form and practice, to make design diversified.
